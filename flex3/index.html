A short life story about a dog called Lucky

ONCE UPON A TIME THEY FOUND ME!

I was born in Buenos Aires. I’m not sure when. My story is a mystery until a a few years back. Somehow I ended up living with a guy at an old building. He dances tango and loves music, but he didn’t have too much time for me. Many people came and went from my house, but I liked nobody.

I was old, skinny and with rotten teeth. However, one wonderful November, a beautiful girl came to my life. She and her mate adopted me, took me to the vet, played with me, and made my life awesome. Now we are a small family in Buenos Aires. I love my humans.

I Love

  1. Eating.
  2. Sleeping 12 hours each day.
  3. take a stroll.

PRO TIP: To all my fans, if you wish to donate tunafish, toys, soft and fuzzy blankets, mice or premium food, do not hesitate to send a check to my house. I live in a high building overlooking the river. My proud owners are María and David.
